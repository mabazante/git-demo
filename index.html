@enigmabtic

I have always enjoyed taking pictures and writing.
It all started with “It’s My Sole Purpose”, another private Instagram account that I used to have.
It had small quotes, poems and also photographs.
I wanted to reinvent it.

Another important influence was my Snapchat.
I began to express myself a lot on this medium in an artisitc way.
I realized at one point that not everyone on my personal account is the correct audience.
It was a playful way to express ideas through abstract photos and quotes combined with emojis.

enigmabtic is a combination of a new stage and also a new mindset in life.
Design courses helped me develop my creative thinking. I became more methodological.
It gave me a different perspective and I use it as a tool to grow more every day.
I also want it to be a medium for others to be empowered to reflect about their own lives too.

The instagram account was created on January 10, 2017. It is an evolving process that will continue to change and develop.
The intention is to use shadows and light, dark and light tones, as well as counterforms to create a compelling composition not only within each photo but also when looking at the overall account.
To have each post interact and come alive with one another is representative of one of the overall messages and is that as humans we all influence one another not only directly but also in very discrete, subconscious ways.

HealthyVibess
Wellness account.
@healthyyvibess

My sister started this account a while back during a summer break.
I would always send her pictures and recipes for her to post.
We would also cook together all the time, so we decided to make a joint account.
Our intention is to promtoe wellness and healthy habits.
Our account is in English, Spanish and occassionally Portuguese.
Our recipes have varied a lot and so have our diets.
They are always made out of wholesome ingredients and avoid processed foods.
These are some examples: plant-based pancakes, "Chicken Salad" Vegan Chickpea Sandwich, Oatmeal&Nicecream Bowl, Energy Balls

We try learn as much as we can from others and from online sources.
We want others to be motivated to take care of themselves and others.
Currently, we are focusing on plant-based diets.
Our philosophy is based off of feeling happy and content with one's own body and mind.
